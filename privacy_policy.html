Privacy Policy — Last updated: March 18, 2026
🔒 100% Local · No Data Collected · No Servers

Overview

LunaApp is a personal feminine health tracker built with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information — which, in short, we don't collect any. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

LunaApp does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Health and medical data (cycle information, symptoms, journal entries)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other user-generated content

How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter — cycle dates, symptoms, journal entries, reminders, health notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

Android Permissions

LunaApp requests only the permissions needed for core functionality:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send reminder notifications you have configured
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to reschedule reminders after device restart
  • VIBRATE / WAKE_LOCK — to deliver notifications reliably

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

Third-Party Services

LunaApp does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no SDKs from Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook, or similar platforms.

Children's Privacy

LunaApp is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete everything at any time by uninstalling the App or via Android Settings → Apps → LunaApp → Clear Data. This permanently removes all information from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and notify users via an App update.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Google Play Store listing page.
